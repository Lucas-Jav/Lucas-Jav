## About me

I am a Full Stack Software Engineer with 3+ years of professional experience building web applications, browser automation, RPA systems and API integrations.

I currently work remotely for a US veterinary technology company, developing TypeScript, Node.js and Playwright automations for catalogue synchronisation, price and stock updates, order placement, purchase history, invoices and tracking across 10+ supplier portals.

Previously, I progressed from Junior Front-End Developer to Tech Lead within 16 months at Leadtax, where I contributed to more than 30 production RPAs for healthcare operations.

- Building reliable browser automation and event-driven workflows
- Experienced with production debugging and root-cause analysis
- Former Tech Lead with code review and delivery-planning experience
- B.Sc. in Computer Science — expected graduation in December 2026
- Based in Brazil and open to relocation
